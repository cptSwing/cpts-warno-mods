## YIMBY - Yes, in my backyard

#### Better Protection and Stealth for Infantry in  Large Buildings / Small Buildings / Ruins

###### `DamageModifierPerFamilyAndResistance` (Infantry) reduced by 0.05

So, damage dealt to infantry in buildings is multiplied by 0.45 instead of 0.5, for example.

###### `ConcealmentBonus` boosted by 0.25

As a result, Concealment multiplied by 3.25 instead of 3, as per this statement by a Eugen dev: *"The concealment of a unit is `UnitConcealmentBonus * TerrainConcealmentBonus / NoiseConcealmentMalus`"*
